About Adaeze Mary Onyenemere

Adaeze Mary Onyenemere is a published Author, Poet, Skill Coach, Entrepreneur, and Personal Development Coach. She is passionate about raising the Next Generation of Job Creators. She teaches individuals a skill called “DIY Printing Mastery” and how to profit from it. She helps people build their self-esteem, improve themselves, and grow their knowledge bank.

Adaeze Mary Onyenemere is a Creative Director at EverGreen Gift Store(EGGS) where she creates graphic & video content. she also crafts, customizes and sell gift items to individuals seeking to appreciate their friends/loved ones with gifts that would remain evergreen in their hearts.

She would love to know you better, you can connect with her on this space by following any of her social media handles. You are loved❤️ #PenofLiberty
